As a digital health company, meeting regulatory and policy objectives are vital to Huma. 
We align our internal policies and processes to ensure compliance with globally recognised standards. We also ensure we employ state of the art tools and techniques to maintain a reliable and compliant framework.

To help you with meeting compliance and reporting requirements, we share information, best practices, and easy access to documentation. Our products and services regularly undergo independent verification of security, privacy, and compliance controls, achieving certifications against global standards to earn your trust. We're constantly working to expand our coverage.

This site contains information about our certifications and compliance standards, as well as general information about certain region or sector-specific regulations.
